John Felix Anthony Cena (/sinə/ SEE-nə; born April 23, 1977)[8] is an American actor, rapper and professional wrestler. He has been signed to WWE since 2001, but has performed part-time since 2018. A record 16-time world champion as recognized by WWE,[a] he is regarded as one of the greatest professional wrestlers of all time.[9][10][11]
